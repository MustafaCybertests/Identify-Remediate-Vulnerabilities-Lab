<h1>Identify & Remediate Vulnerabilities Lab</h1>

<h2>Description</h2>
This Project consists of using a Virtual Machine, in this scenario METASPOLOITABLE, to help train my red team skills. It is important to be able to communicate effectively in the field of Cyber Security, you have to possess the skill of relaying information and breaking down and explaining technical concepts to non-technical clients and you can do just that by identifying and remediating vulnurabilities.
Our objectives is to sign up for Metasploitable, using Nmap to scan the machine, and Imagining this is a client's machine thats been exposed to the internet. I will provide my findings & remediation steps

<h2>Languages and Utilities Used</h2>

- <b>PowerShell</b> 
- <b>Diskpart</b>

<h2>Environments Used </h2>

- <b>Windows 10</b> (21H2)
